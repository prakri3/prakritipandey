About Me

Hello! I am Prakriti Pandey, a civil engineer from Nepal. I am currently pursuing my master's degree and conducting research on mobility related to motorcycles. My research interests include micromobility, mobility space in cities, and urban areas.

Projects

Assessing Road Surface Quality for Two-Wheelers

This project involves developing a novel ride comfort index for motorcycles using frequency response functions and road surface profiles. Data was collected using accelerometers on urban roads in Hokkaido, Japan.

Micromobility in Dense Urban Areas

A study exploring shared e-scooter usage patterns in high-density urban areas like Shibuya, Tokyo. This research used GPS trajectory data and surveyed rider behavior to recommend improved parking zones and route safety measures.
